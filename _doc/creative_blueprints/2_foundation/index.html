The Problem

Omni HR — like every HRIS — is a system of record. It stores the data, but a human still has to notice the payroll anomaly, chase the missing statutory doc, unblock the new hire, and catch the attrition cluster.

Across five jurisdictions, that work is endless, error-prone, and invisible until something breaks.

→

The Solution

Mino Autopilot is the action layer on top of Omni. A coordinated team of HR agents detects issues, recommends actions, routes approvals, and executes workflows — across payroll, onboarding, compliance, retention, and performance.

Nothing sensitive ships without human approval. Every step is auditable. That's the trust moat.

Omni doesn't just store HR data anymore — it runs HR operations safely with AI.
