# Mino Autopilot — Product Overview

> The single source of truth for what Mino Autopilot is. Keep current as product facts change.

## One-liner

**Mino Autopilot is the agentic HR operating layer for Omni HR — it turns Omni from a system of record into a system of action.**

## What it is

Mino Autopilot is an agentic HR command center built on top of Omni HR. It does not just store HR data; it watches it, detects issues, recommends actions, routes approvals, and safely executes workflows across payroll, onboarding, compliance, retention, and performance.

It is **not a chatbot**. It is a coordinated team of specialized HR agents working behind the scenes, surfacing "what HR should act on today" and executing only what a human has approved.

## Positioning

- **Category:** Agentic HR operations layer (system of action), distinct from HRIS / system of record.
- **Against the status quad:** Most HR software stores and reports. Omni stores the data; Mino runs the operations on top of it.
- **The wedge:** Omni already owns the HR data. The defensible move is the *action layer* — safe, approved execution — not another assistant.
- **The trust moat:** Every sensitive action passes through a human approval gate and lands in a transparent audit log. Safety is the product, not a feature.

## Target users

- **Primary persona — Sarah, HR Operations Lead** at a mid-size, multi-country company. Drowning in payroll close, compliance deadlines, onboarding blockers, and review chasing across five jurisdictions.
- **HR / People leaders** who need confidence that AI won't act recklessly.
- **Finance partners** who consume payroll explanations and packets.
- **Buyers / investors** evaluating Omni's move from record to action.

A realistic **340-person company** operating across **Singapore, Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, and Hong Kong**. Mock data only — no real integrations. Includes sample employees, teams, managers, payroll items, onboarding tasks, compliance documents, and approval records.

## The agent scaffold (how it works)

A set of specialized HR agents coordinate behind the scenes — never acting randomly, always within bounds:

- **Payroll Agent** — detects payroll anomalies and variances, prepares the payroll close packet.
- **Compliance Agent** — tracks statutory deadlines, missing documents, and country-specific risk.
- **Onboarding Agent** — drives new-hire readiness and clears blockers before day one.
- **Retention Agent** — surfaces team-level attrition risk clusters (never creepy individual surveillance).
- **Performance Agent** — chases reviews and supports calibration.
- **Approval Routing Agent** — routes the right decision to the right human approver.
- **Audit Agent** — records every action, the data accessed, the reasoning, and the human approval.

Each agent exposes: **Goal · Inputs · Reasoning summary · Suggested action · Required approval level · Execution status.**

## Core surfaces (navigation)

1. **Today** — dashboard of "what HR should act on today": prioritized agent recommendations grouped Critical / Important / Suggested. Each card shows issue, AI explanation, impact, suggested action, confidence score, approval status, and CTAs (Review · Approve · Ask Mino).
2. **Agent Missions** — mission control to launch agentic workflows (Payroll Close, New Hire Launch, Compliance Watch, Attrition Prevention, Performance Calibration, Workforce Planning). Each shows what it does, estimated time saved, risk level, last run. Launching shows a step-by-step agent progress timeline.
3. **Payroll Close** — flagship flow. Multi-country payroll analysis with country-by-country summary, headcount / salary / overtime / expense variance, leave impact, compliance flags, a suggested Finance explanation, and an approval checklist. CTAs: "Approve payroll packet" and "Ask Mino to explain".
4. **Onboarding** — new hires starting soon with status, blockers, documents, equipment, training, and recommended next actions.
5. **Compliance** — statutory deadlines, missing documents, country-specific risk, policy gaps, severity, owner, deadline, suggested remediation across all five countries.
6. **Retention** — team-level people-risk page: risk clusters, engagement trend, leave-pattern changes, manager changes, suggested interventions and HR action plans.
7. **Approvals** — inbox of agent-proposed actions with reasoning, data used, risk level, and approver. Buttons: Approve · Reject · Request Changes.
8. **Audit Log** — transparent trail: timestamp, agent action, data accessed, reasoning summary, human approval, final action taken.
9. **Settings.**

Plus an **Ask Mino** assistant drawer available across the app — an HR chief-of-staff that answers concisely and structured, always with suggested next actions.

## The safety model (non-negotiable)

Mino **never executes a sensitive HR action without human approval.** The approval gate plus the audit log are the foundation of enterprise trust. This is the emotional core of the product: *"Omni doesn't just store HR data anymore — it runs HR operations safely with AI."*

## Hero demo flow

Open Today → see payroll anomaly + onboarding blocker → launch Payroll Close Mission → Mino runs the agentic workflow → produces the payroll packet → user reviews reasoning + audit trail → approves → Mino marks it complete.
